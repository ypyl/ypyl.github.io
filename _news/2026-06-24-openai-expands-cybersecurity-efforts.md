date: 2026-06-24

OpenAI has released **GPT-5.5-Cyber**, a specialized model for cybersecurity, alongside an update to the **Codex Security** plugin. The model is available only to verified users and is tuned for lower refusal rates on security-specific queries. The updated Codex Security scans codebases, analyzes attack vectors, checks reachability of vulnerable code, and generates patches, with support for external scanner bug reports and SARIF/CodeQL export.

In partnership with **HackerOne** and **Trail of Bits**, OpenAI also launched **Patch the Planet**, an initiative for automated protection of open-source projects. cURL, Python, and Go have already joined the program.
