Director Neill Blomkamp (*District 9*) has released **Nightborne**, a 13-minute sci-fi horror short film created entirely using the **Seedance 2.0** video generation model, styled as a documentary about a military programme reviving fallen soldiers. Blomkamp directed frame-by-frame through prompts based on concept art, with the studio licensing biometric data from **32 individuals** for faces and voice to avoid copyright issues. The filmmaker has announced plans for a full-length AI feature and opened a new studio, **Barley Studios**, focused on neural-network production to replace his classical Oats Studios.
