date: 2026-07-21

categories: news
---

Google has released three new Gemini models: **Gemini 3.6 Flash** uses 17% fewer output tokens than its predecessor and costs $1.5/M input and $7.5/M output tokens; **Gemini 3.5 Flash-Lite** targets high-volume, low-latency tasks at 350 tokens/second for $0.30/M input tokens; and **Gemini 3.5 Flash Cyber** is a specialised model for vulnerability discovery and remediation — restricted to governments and trusted partners in a limited pilot, not publicly released. Google also confirmed that **Gemini 3.5 Pro** is being tested with partners and that training for **Gemini 4** — described as the company's most extensive effort to date — has already begun.
