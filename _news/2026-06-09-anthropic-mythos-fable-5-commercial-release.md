Anthropic is on the verge of releasing **Claude Fable 5**—a public adaptation of the **Mythos** architecture developed under the secretive **Project Glasswing**. The original Mythos model can autonomously discover zero-day vulnerabilities and execute multi-step cyberattacks. To bring the technology to market safely, Anthropic will split the release: the base Mythos remains exclusive to governments and vetted partners, while **Fable 5** becomes a publicly accessible flagship with strict system-level guardrails on network penetration and malicious code execution—while retaining Mythos's advanced reasoning, ultra-long context, and multi-step autonomous task capabilities. The official announcement is expected on June 10 at the [Code with Claude conference in Tokyo](https://claude.com/code-with-claude/tokyo).
