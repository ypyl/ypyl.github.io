title: "Moonshot Pauses Kimi K3 Subscriptions Due to Compute Shortage, Splits into Two Tiers"
date: 2026-07-21
tags: news
categories: news
---

Chinese AI startup Moonshot has temporarily suspended new subscriptions for its **Kimi K3** model due to insufficient compute capacity, while existing users continue without restrictions. To balance cluster load, the company is splitting the single subscription into two tiers: **Kimi Membership** for web, mobile, and standard use cases, and **Kimi Code Membership** with dedicated resources for code generation and analysis. New customer access will resume as infrastructure scales.
