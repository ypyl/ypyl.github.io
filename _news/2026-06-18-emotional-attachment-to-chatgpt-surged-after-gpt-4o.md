title: "Emotional Attachment to ChatGPT Surged After GPT-4o, Reddit Study Finds"
date: 2026-06-18
---

Researchers behind the **rchatgpt-pulse** project analyzed 137,154 r/ChatGPT posts spanning December 2022 to November 2025 and found that emotional dependence on the chatbot—users seeking psychological support and forming attachments—began rising almost immediately after **GPT-4o** launched. Their **PuLSE** monitoring system could have detected the trend months before it entered public discourse. For GPT-5, emotional engagement appeared in at least 30.5% of complaints about the model, sharply diverging from OpenAI's own estimate of 1.9%, which the authors argue shows usage frequency alone masks the true scale of impact. OpenAI's GPT-4o system card had already flagged risks of anthropomorphization and emotional dependency, and Sam Altman later confirmed the company has been tracking mental-health-related use for roughly a year.
