AI-generated content now accounts for roughly **25%** of all social media posts over 250 words, according to a study by AI-detection platform **Pangram**, which analyzed over 1 million publications. **LinkedIn** leads with 41% of long-form posts written entirely by AI, while on **X** only 52.7% of tweets are purely human-authored. The most authentic platform is **Reddit**—though 11.6% of original posts are AI-generated, a striking **98.1% of comments** are written by real people.
