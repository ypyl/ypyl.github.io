title: "Anthropic Suspends Fable 5 and Mythos 5 Access Following US Export Control Directive"
date: 2026-06-12
---

The US government has issued an export control directive requiring Anthropic to **suspend all access to Fable 5 and Mythos 5** for any foreign national — including Anthropic's own foreign employees — citing national security authorities. Anthropic was forced to abruptly disable both models for **all customers** to ensure compliance, though access to all other Claude models remains unaffected.

Anthropic states the government's concern stems from a narrow, non-universal jailbreak technique that the company reviewed and found to reveal only minor, previously known vulnerabilities — capabilities Anthropic says are widely available in other publicly-accessible models. The company disagrees with the decision, calling it a misunderstanding, and warns that applying this standard across the industry would essentially halt all new model deployments. Anthropic is working to restore access as soon as possible.
