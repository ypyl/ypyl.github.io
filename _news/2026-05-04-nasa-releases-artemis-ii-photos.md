title: "NASA Releases Over 12,000 High-Resolution Photos from Artemis II Mission"
date: 2026-05-04
---

NASA has made publicly available more than 12,000 high-resolution photographs taken during the Artemis II lunar mission, which took place earlier in April. A total of 12,217 images were captured during the mission, offering an extensive visual record.

All photos can be viewed and downloaded from NASA's official website.

[View the Artemis II photos](https://eol.jsc.nasa.gov/SearchPhotos/ShowQueryResults-Lightcycle.pl?results=1777772559122361)
