title: "US Lifts Export Restrictions on Claude Fable 5 and Mythos 5"
date: 2026-07-01

The US Department of Commerce has officially lifted the previously imposed export restrictions on **Claude Fable 5** and **Mythos 5**. Anthropic confirmed on X that full access to both models will be restored starting tomorrow. The company thanked users for their patience during the forced service suspension and expressed gratitude to all specialists who assisted in the redeployment of systems. Anthropic also indicated that patch notes with additional details and future roadmap plans for the model lineup will be released soon.
