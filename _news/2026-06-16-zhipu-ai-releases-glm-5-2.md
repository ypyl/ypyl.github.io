Zhipu AI (international brand ZAi) has released **GLM-5.2**, expanding the context window from 200K to **1 million tokens**. The model is available to all GLM Coding Plans users and offers two modes: **High Mode** for fast code generation and **Max Mode** with chain-of-thought reasoning for long-term planning. It is optimized for integration with popular AI agents including Cline, Roo Code, OpenCode CLI, and Claude Code. Usage consumes platform quotas at a 3x multiplier during peak hours and 2x off-peak, with a promotional 1x rate for off-peak hours until the end of September 2026. The company plans to release the weights on Hugging Face soon.
