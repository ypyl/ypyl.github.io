AI detection platform **Pangram** analyzed over **1 million social media posts** and found that **25% of posts over 250 words** are written entirely by AI. **LinkedIn** leads with 41% of long-form posts and 30% of shorter posts being fully AI-generated, while on **X** only 52.7% of tweets are written by humans. **Medium** (33%) and **Substack** (22%) also show significant AI penetration, while **Reddit** remains the most authentic platform with 98.1% of comments written by real people.
