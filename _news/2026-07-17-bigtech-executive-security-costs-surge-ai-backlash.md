Growing public backlash over AI development has translated into real security risks for the tech sector. According to Liferaft analysts, threats against data centers and AI company leaders have increased **7x**, driving a sharp rise in corporate security budgets — 38% of S&P 500 companies reported executive protection expenses in 2025, up from 27% in 2021. Palantir's spending on executive security jumped **150%** year-over-year, Oracle's rose **86%**, and Salesforce's budget reached **$4 million**. Security agencies in Silicon Valley report surging demand for armed yet discreet bodyguards, while AI company management strongly advises employees against wearing branded clothing off-campus.
