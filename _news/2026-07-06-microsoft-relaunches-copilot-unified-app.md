Microsoft is preparing a major **Copilot overhaul** in August, merging its consumer and enterprise versions into a single unified application. The update introduces **AutoPilot**—background agents for automating routine tasks—alongside new code-generation tools, all behind a paywall. The company is shutting down experimental features like **Copilot Podcasts** and **Labs** as it shifts focus from showcasing AI capabilities to solving practical problems. According to an internal memo from a Microsoft VP, the revamped Copilot still needs to **"earn its right to exist."**
