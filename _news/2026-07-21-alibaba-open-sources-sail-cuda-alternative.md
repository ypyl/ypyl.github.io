Alibaba's semiconductor arm T-Head has announced it will open source the **SAIL** software stack for its proprietary **Zhenwu** AI accelerators, positioning it as an open alternative to Nvidia's CUDA ecosystem. Announced at the World AI Conference, T-Head claims SAIL can be adapted to popular ML frameworks in under 7 days. The flagship Zhenwu **M890** processor, unveiled in May and designed for AI agent workloads, has already shipped over 560,000 units to hundreds of enterprise customers.
