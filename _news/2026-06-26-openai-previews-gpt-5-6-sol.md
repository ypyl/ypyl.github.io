OpenAI has previewed **GPT-5.6 Sol**—a new flagship model alongside two variants: **Terra** (a balanced model for everyday tasks) and **Luna** (a fast, low-cost option). Sol is purpose-built for complex agentic workloads, coding, biology, and cybersecurity, introducing two new reasoning modes: **max** for deeper deliberation and **ultra** which orchestrates sub-agents for intricate multi-step processes.

The release is currently in a limited preview for trusted partners via the **API** and **Codex**, with a broader rollout across ChatGPT, Codex, and API expected later.
