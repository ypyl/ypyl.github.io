Meta's **Superintelligence Labs** has introduced **Muse Image** for image generation and a preview of **Muse Video**, both built on the Muse architecture. Muse Image pairs with the **Muse Spark** language model to support editing, sketching, annotations, text rendering, and real-time search-driven reference blending—users can even `@mention` a public account to replicate its visual style. The early Muse Video build generates video and audio in a single stream with precise synchronization, and all outputs are protected by a **Content Seal** watermark resistant to compression and cropping. Muse Image is available for free with daily limits, with a paid pro tier, while the video generator is expected to launch in the coming months.
