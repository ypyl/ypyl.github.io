Researchers have unveiled **Phantom Twist**, a single-motor drone whose entire body spins at up to **25 rotations per second** during flight, rendering it nearly invisible due to motion blur. An AI generated and simulated 20,000 design variants against real-world backgrounds to arrive at the final configuration — a coaxial design where the propeller and frame counter-rotate with no static parts. The drone is **10 times harder to spot** than a conventional quadcopter, with the team positioning it for wildlife monitoring and infrastructure inspection.
