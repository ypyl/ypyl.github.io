xAI has released **Grok 4.5**, a new model built for coding and agentic tasks, developed in collaboration with the **Cursor** team—the editor that moved under SpaceX control in June. On **Terminal Bench 2.1**, Grok 4.5 nearly matches GPT-5.5 and trails Fable 5 by just one point, though it lags behind leaders on GitHub-based problem solving (DeepSWE 1.1).

The model is priced at **$2/1M input tokens** and **$6/1M output tokens**, with a generation speed of up to **80 tokens/second**. According to xAI, Grok 4.5 uses **4.2× fewer tokens** than Opus 4.8 on SWE Bench Pro. It is available in the xAI console, Grok Build, and the Cursor editor, with official plugins released for **Word, PowerPoint, and Excel**. The EU release is delayed until mid-July.
