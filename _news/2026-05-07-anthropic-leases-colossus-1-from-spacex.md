title: "Anthropic Leases Colossus 1 from SpaceX and Raises Claude Limits"
date: 2026-05-07
---

**Anthropic** has contracted the full compute capacity of **SpaceX's Colossus 1**—over 300 MW and more than 220,000 NVIDIA GPUs—adding to existing deals with Amazon, Google/Broadcom, Microsoft/NVIDIA, and Fluidstack. The added capacity has been converted into expanded limits for **Claude Code** and the **Claude API**:

- **Claude Code**: five-hour limits are now doubled for Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise tiers; Pro and Max peak-hour restrictions have also been removed.
- **Claude API**: Opus limits are raised across all tiers, with Tier 4 now supporting up to **10M input tokens** and **800K output tokens per minute**.

Colossus 1 capacity will be prioritized for **Claude Pro and Max** subscribers. The companies also expressed interest in deploying gigawatt-scale AI nodes in orbit, though this remains a declaration rather than a signed agreement.
