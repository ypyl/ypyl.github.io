title: "Microsoft Unveils Majorana 2 Quantum Chip, Targets 2029 for Full Quantum Computer"
date: 2026-06-03
---

A year after the debut of Majorana 1, Satya Nadella has unveiled **Majorana 2**, Microsoft's next-generation scalable quantum processor. The company claims the new chip delivers qubits that are roughly **1,000 times more accurate** than its predecessor, enabled by a novel lead-based material. Microsoft is now targeting **2029** as the timeline for building a fully functional quantum computer.
