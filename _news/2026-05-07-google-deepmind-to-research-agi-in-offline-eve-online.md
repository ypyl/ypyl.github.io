title: "Google DeepMind to Research AGI in Offline EVE Online"
date: 2026-05-07
---

**Google DeepMind** has partnered with **Fenris Creations** to use **EVE Online** as a sandbox for AGI research. The experiments will run on isolated offline instances, separate from the live Tranquility server, testing AI in a complex environment with real player-like dynamics. More details are expected to be revealed at **Fanfest 2026** next week. The announcement coincides with the studio's independence from Pearl Abyss and its rebrand to Fenris Creations.
