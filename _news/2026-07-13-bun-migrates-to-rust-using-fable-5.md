date: 2026-07-13
tags: news
categories: news
---

Bun creator **Jarred Sumner** has migrated the JavaScript runtime from Zig to **Rust in just 11 days**, using 64 parallel instances of Anthropic's **Fable 5** model to generate over **1 million lines of code**. Sumner estimates the manual rewrite would have taken about a year; the API bill came to **$165,000**, covered by Anthropic, which acquired Bun in December 2025. The new codebase is available in Bun v1.4.0 (canary), fixes **128 known bugs**, and delivers a **2–5% performance improvement**.
