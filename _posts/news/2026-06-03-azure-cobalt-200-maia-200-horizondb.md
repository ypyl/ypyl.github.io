Microsoft announced several cloud infrastructure updates, including **Azure Cobalt 200** virtual machines delivering a 50% performance boost and fully optimized for modern agentic AI workloads, alongside the second-generation **Maia 200** accelerator. The company also introduced **Azure HorizonDB**, an enterprise-ready PostgreSQL engine purpose-built for the AI era, and previewed **Foundry Local** on Azure Local, enabling sovereign AI deployments that keep data within a customer's own environment.
