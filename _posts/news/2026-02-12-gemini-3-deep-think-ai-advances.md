Google's Gemini 3 Deep Think AI model demonstrates remarkable capabilities in solving high-level problems typically tackled by top programmers and Olympiad participants. The model achieves state-of-the-art results on the ARC-AGI-2 abstract reasoning test and excels in Humanity’s Last Exam, which features advanced math, science, and engineering challenges. It also reaches a strong competitive programming level with a 3455 Elo rating on Codeforces and achieves gold standard results in the 2025 Physics and Chemistry Olympiad written exams. Gemini 3 Deep Think can analyze complex technical problems, assist in research, evaluate engineering solutions, and serve as a partner for deep analytical tasks.
