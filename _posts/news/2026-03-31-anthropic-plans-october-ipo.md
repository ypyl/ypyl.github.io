Anthropic is preparing for an initial public offering (IPO) expected to take place in October 2026. The company is in talks with major Wall Street firms including Goldman Sachs, JPMorgan, and Morgan Stanley to select underwriters for the listing. Following a $30 billion funding round led by GIC and Coatue earlier this year, Anthropic's valuation has surged to $380 billion. Market interest is already evident, with ETF issuers filing to register margin funds linked to the company's anticipated stock.
