Anthropic, the creator of **Claude**, has submitted a confidential draft S-1 registration to the SEC, taking the first formal step toward going public. The timing, share volume, and price range have not yet been set — the listing depends on market conditions.

The filing follows a **$65 billion funding round** that pushed the company's valuation close to **$1 trillion**. Anthropic is legally structured as a **Public Benefit Corporation**, which requires it to balance shareholder returns with a commitment to public good.
