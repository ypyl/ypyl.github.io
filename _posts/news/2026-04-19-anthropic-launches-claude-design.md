Anthropic has introduced **Claude Design**, a new product designed for generating ready-made layouts, prototypes, slides, and websites. Powered by their strongest model, **Claude Opus 4.7**, which features advanced visual understanding, the tool automatically extracts the design system from your codebase and design files to maintain brand consistency without manual input.

Currently available as a research preview for Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise plans, Claude Design aims to streamline the design process by keeping all elements within brand guidelines such as fonts, colors, and spacing.

Try it at [claude.ai/design](https://claude.ai/design).
