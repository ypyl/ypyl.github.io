Microsoft and GitHub have introduced **Exam GH-600 Agentic AI Developer (beta)** — an official certification test for building and managing AI-powered development workflows. The 120-minute exam assesses skills in managing AI agents, their memory, Copilot workflows, and AI-augmented software development lifecycles.

Key details:
- **Focus areas**: AI-assisted software development, internal AI agent workflows, multi-agent orchestration, human-in-the-loop AI systems, and managing AI for secure project execution.
- **Cost**: $165 via Pearson Vue, with an **80% discount** for the first 100 candidates using voucher **GH600Flanders**.
- **Availability**: The beta exam is currently unavailable in India, Pakistan, Turkey, and China.
- **Timeline**: Beta launched in May 2026, with results published ~8 weeks after the beta period ends. General availability is expected in **July 2026**.

Microsoft has also released a collection of study materials to help candidates prepare for the certification.

[Exam GH-600 Agentic AI Developer (beta)](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/certifications/exams/gh-600/)
