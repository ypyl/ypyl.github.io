Chinese autonomous driving firms Baidu's Apollo Go and WeRide have halted robotaxi operations in Dubai following Iran's missile strikes that heightened regional tensions. While WeRide continues services in Abu Dhabi and Riyadh, both companies emphasized safety and compliance with local authorities. The suspensions highlight the impact of geopolitical instability on emerging mobility technologies.
