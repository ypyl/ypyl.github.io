Field tests conducted in China demonstrated a military drone's remarkable accuracy, achieving nearly 100% hit rate on a standard target at 100 meters while hovering at 10 meters. Unlike previous reports that combined specialized drones and custom firearms, this test used a standard assault rifle adopted by the Chinese army. This development highlights significant advancements in drone-based precision shooting capabilities.
