Google has introduced the **Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite**, the fastest and most affordable model in the Gemini 3 series. Priced at just $0.25 per million input tokens and $1.50 per million output tokens, the model delivers a 45% speed increase over Gemini 2.5 Flash while significantly reducing costs.

Key features include adjustable "thinking" levels for varying task complexity and optimization for high-scale applications like UI generation, dashboards, simulations, and workflow automation. Developers can access Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite via the Gemini API in Google AI Studio.

For more details, visit [Google's official blog](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/models-and-research/gemini-models/gemini-3-1-flash-lite/).
