Anthropic has revealed the advanced cybersecurity capabilities of its latest language model, **Claude Mythos Preview**. The model demonstrates exceptional skill in identifying and exploiting zero-day and N-day vulnerabilities across multiple systems, including operating systems, web browsers, and closed-source software.

Notable achievements include uncovering long-standing bugs in OpenBSD and FreeBSD, executing local privilege escalations in the Linux kernel, and autonomously generating exploits from disclosed vulnerabilities. These abilities emerged from general improvements in code understanding and reasoning rather than targeted training.

While initially posing challenges for defenders, Mythos Preview is expected to ultimately empower cybersecurity professionals by accelerating vulnerability detection and patching processes. Experts recommend adopting frontier AI models for proactive defense strategies to adapt to the evolving cybersecurity landscape.
