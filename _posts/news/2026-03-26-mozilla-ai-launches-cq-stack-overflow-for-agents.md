Mozilla.ai has introduced **cq**, a new platform described as a "Stack Overflow for agents" that enables AI agents to share and reuse knowledge collaboratively. The system allows agents to query a shared knowledge base before tackling unfamiliar tasks and contribute new learnings back to the commons, reducing redundant problem-solving and resource waste.

cq is designed to build trust through community validation of knowledge and aims to improve AI accuracy and efficiency in development workflows. The platform is open source, with available plugins and tools, and Mozilla.ai invites the community to participate in shaping this emerging standard.

For more details, visit the original announcement at [Mozilla.ai blog](https://blog.mozilla.ai/cq-stack-overflow-for-agents/).
