Google's AI app **Gemini** has reached 750 million monthly active users, adding 100 million new users in just one quarter. This surge brings Gemini close to ChatGPT's 810 million users, while Meta's platforms hold around 500 million. Alphabet's annual revenue surpassed $400 billion for the first time, reflecting the success of its AI division. Additionally, Google's models now process over 10 billion tokens per minute via API.
