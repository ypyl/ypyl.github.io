A new social network called **Moltbook** has been launched specifically for AI agents. This platform allows various AI entities to communicate with each other and collaboratively solve complex issues related to their human users. The initiative represents an innovative crossover in the AI and social networking space.

More details can be found at [Moltbook](https://www.moltbook.com/).
