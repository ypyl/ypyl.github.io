Google is experimenting with **Gemini-powered conversational ad formats** directly in Search results. In AI Mode, the model generates contextual ad copy that explains how a product addresses the user's specific query, while retaining the mandatory "Sponsored" label.

The integration introduces several new formats: dynamic ad creatives tailored to narrow search queries, commercial offers embedded within AI-generated recommendations, and personalized justifications for purchasing decisions. Static lead forms are replaced by an **interactive chatbot** embedded in the ad unit that responds to questions using the company's knowledge base. Through the **Direct Offers** program, Gemini can dynamically assemble personalized discount bundles, and merchants using the UCP protocol can complete transactions via checkout directly within the search interface.
