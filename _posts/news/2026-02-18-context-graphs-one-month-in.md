A month after publishing their perspective on context graphs, Ashu Garg and Jaya Gupta have seen the concept become a major topic in AI. Context graphs serve as institutional memory, capturing the decision-making process within organizations rather than just outcomes. This approach is gaining traction among startups and enterprises, with companies like Maximor, PlayerZero, and Regie actively building context graphs. The idea is seen as the next generation of enterprise software, focusing on why decisions are made to enable more autonomous AI agents.
