OpenAI has launched **GPT-5.3 Instant**, a major update to its most widely used model, focusing on enhanced communication quality. The model now declines safe requests less often and avoids overly cautious moralizing responses. Significant improvements include deeper synthesis of web search results with internal knowledge and a notable reduction in hallucinations—down 26.8% with web search and 19.7% relying solely on internal data.

Text generation is livelier and more stylistically diverse. GPT-5.3 Instant is available to all users, including via API under the identifier `gpt-5.3-chat-latest`. The previous GPT-5.2 Instant version will remain in legacy access for paying subscribers until June 3, 2026.
