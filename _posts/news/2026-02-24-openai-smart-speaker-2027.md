OpenAI plans to release a smart speaker with a built-in camera and facial recognition capabilities in February 2027. The device, priced between $200 and $300, will analyze the surroundings and provide proactive advice. Production will be handled by Foxconn as part of a new ecosystem including smart glasses, a wireless headset, a smart stylus, and a desk lamp prototype.
