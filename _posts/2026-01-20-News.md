5. Google revealed the main bottleneck for new data centers is physical connection to the transmission grid, with wait times up to 10-12 years for interconnection. AI growth now limited by grid capacity, permitting, and construction delays, not GPUs or power contracts. By 2026, securing megawatt-scale grid connections will be the critical constraint for AI scaling.
